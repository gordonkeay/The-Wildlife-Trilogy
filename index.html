The Author

I grew up in Grimsby and ended up on the shore of Lac Léman in Switzerland. Between those two points there is a life — forty five years of wildlife, of searching for something I couldn't name until I stepped off a dock and found it beneath my feet.

The Wildlife Trilogy is the result of that search. Not a finished account of a life understood — a living document of a life in the process of understanding itself. Three books, three registers, one question running through all of them:

What does it mean to move through the world without resistance?

I am a personal trainer, a pump foiler, a bread baker, a student of living systems, and the founder of the Archeus Planare Foundation on Lac Léman. I write in the small hours before anyone else is awake, by candlelight, with a pen that has a bent nib.
